About Alec's Towing

Your trusted partner on the road and off

Founded with a commitment to exceptional service, Alec's Towing & Off-Road Recovery LLC has been serving our community with reliable, prompt, and professional towing and recovery services for over 10 years.

Our team consists of certified professionals with extensive experience in both standard roadside towing and specialized off-road recovery operations. We invest in state-of-the-art equipment and continuous training to ensure we can handle any situation safely and efficiently.
